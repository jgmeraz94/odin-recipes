Grandma's World Famous Home Lasagna!

Today you will learn how to make Grandma's world famous home lasagna to cook for the family! It is so good you will surely impress everyone at the table and leave everyone happy.

This rich lasagna dish is topped with a rich tomato sauce with rich spices and seasonings to really compliment the delicious layers of meat and cheese of the lasagna. Each layer consisting of ground beef and Italian sausage. Each layer topped with a three cheese blend of mozzarella, parmesan, and ricotta. Hope you are ready to make this delicious home dish that will really wow everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
